# Product Context

## What is DLM?

**Doorstep Laundry Marketplace** — a modern, youth-focused, mobile-first marketplace where customers find and book nearby laundries for pickup + delivery. Partners get tools to run their business. Admins oversee the network.

## North-star metric

**Weekly Active Customers** with at least one completed order in the past 7 days.

## Supporting metrics

- New customer activation (first order placed) within 7 days of signup
- Order completion rate (placed → delivered)
- 7-day customer retention after first order
- Partner monthly active rate (≥ 1 fulfilled order)
- Average customer rating per laundry
- Subscription attach rate

## Personas

### Riya — Customer (25, urban)
- Lives in an apartment. Hates the laundromat trip.
- Phone-first. Loves clean UI.
- Wants reliability + transparency.
- Repeats orders if the experience is good.

### Mahesh — Laundry Partner (38, micro-business)
- Owns a small laundry shop.
- Wants more business.
- Lower tech literacy.
- Needs simple operational tools.

### Anita — Admin (DLM team)
- Approves new partners.
- Resolves complaints.
- Watches health dashboards.

## Differentiators

- **Premium feel** — design quality customers notice.
- **Real-time tracking** — fewer "where is my order?" calls.
- **Subscription** — predictable revenue + lower acquisition cost.
- **Partner tooling** — partners actually want to use the platform.

## Voice

Confident, warm, witty. Not cheesy. First-person where appropriate ("Schedule my pickup").

## What we are NOT

- ❌ A corporate enterprise SaaS
- ❌ A booking aggregator with no quality controls
- ❌ A platform that races to the bottom on price
